About the Game

Ticket to Ride: Europe is a beautifully designed board game where players race to claim train routes across Europe. Build railways, complete destination tickets, and outmaneuver opponents in this fast-paced strategy game. With tunnels, ferries, and train stations adding new layers of challenge, this version of the classic game offers depth and replayability. It’s perfect for families, casual gamers, and anyone who loves maps, planning, and a bit of friendly competition.

Game Features:

  • 2-5 Players
  • 30-60 Minutes Playing Time
  • Ages 8+
  • Gorgeous map of Europe
  • Combines luck, strategy, and planning
