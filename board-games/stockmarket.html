About the Game

Stock Market is an exciting board game that simulates the thrill of investing. Players buy and sell shares, react to market events, and compete to build the most valuable portfolio. The game teaches basic economic principles in a fun, competitive way—perfect for families, finance fans, or game nights. With each round, players must balance risk and reward while trying to outwit the market and each other. Learn, play, and maybe even spark a love for trading!

Game Features:

  • 2-6 Players
  • 45-60 Minutes Playing Time
  • Ages 10+
  • Simulates real-world market dynamics
  • Teaches money management and strategic planning
