About the Game

Catan is a modern classic strategy game where players compete to settle an island by building roads, settlements, and cities. You’ll gather resources like brick, wood, and wheat, then trade with others or strategically expand your territory. Every game is different, thanks to a modular board and unpredictable dice rolls. Catan is perfect for game nights with friends or family and offers endless replayability with its mix of planning, negotiation, and luck.

Game Features:

  • 3–4 Players
  • 60–90 Minutes Playing Time
  • Ages 10+
  • Highly interactive and replayable
  • Encourages negotiation and strategic thinking
