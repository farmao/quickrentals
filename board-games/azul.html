About the Game

Azul is an abstract strategy board game designed by Michael Kiesling and released by Plan B Games. In the game, players take turns drafting colored tiles from suppliers to their player board. Players score points based on how they've placed their tiles to decorate the palace. Extra points are scored for specific patterns and completing sets; wasted supplies harm the player's score.

Game Features:

  • 2-4 Players
  • 30-45 Minutes Playing Time
  • Ages 8+
  • Beautiful artwork and high-quality components
  • Easy to learn, challenging to master
