About the Game

Sequence is a classic board and card game where players aim to create rows of five chips on the board by playing matching cards from their hand. It’s easy to learn, but the mix of luck and strategy keeps things interesting every time. Block your opponents, build your own sequences, and use the jokers (the Jacks!) wisely. Great for both kids and adults, Sequence is perfect for family game nights and casual play.

Game Features:

  • 2–12 Players (in teams)
  • 20-45 Minutes Playing Time
  • Ages 7+
  • Combines cards, strategy, and board play
  • Quick to learn, highly replayable
