Item description

The Portronics Beem 480 is a compact and efficient LED projector designed for immersive home entertainment and casual professional use. Supporting Full HD 1080p resolution, it delivers sharp visuals and rich colors for an engaging viewing experience. Capable of projecting up to 150 inches, it turns any plain wall into a dynamic screen, perfect for movie nights, gaming, or presentations.

Its built-in stereo speakers offer clear sound, while a range of connectivity options—including HDMI, USB, AV, and 3.5mm audio out—ensure compatibility with a wide array of devices like laptops, smartphones, streaming sticks, and consoles. The Beem 480 is built for durability and energy efficiency.

Lightweight and easy to set up, it includes a remote control and intuitive interface for seamless operation. Whether you're setting up an impromptu screening or traveling with a portable presentation tool, the Beem 480 balances performance, portability, and affordability in a sleek, user-friendly design.
