The Portronics Dash 10 is a powerful and portable Bluetooth party speaker designed to bring immersive sound to any setting. The built-in RGB LED lights enhance the party vibe, syncing with your music for a dynamic visual experience.

Sturdy and stylish, the speaker is housed in a rugged, portable design with a comfortable handle, making it easy to carry around. Whether you're indoors or outside, the Portronics Dash 10 offers reliable performance, versatility, and booming sound that fills the space.
